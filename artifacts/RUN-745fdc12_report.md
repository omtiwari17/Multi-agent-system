# Supplier Report: Injection Molding (ABS, India, High Volume)

## Introduction
This report provides an analysis and ranking of potential suppliers for injection molding services. The search criteria focused on suppliers capable of **Injection Molding** using **ABS** material, located in **India**, holding **ISO 9001** certification, and with a minimum monthly production capacity of **50,000 units**.

## Executive Summary
Three suppliers were identified that largely meet the core requirements: Superb Plastics Pvt. Ltd., Precision Molds & Plastics, and Plastikon Industries India. All operate in India, offer ABS injection molding, and are ISO 9001 certified. Differentiations arise in the explicitness of stated capacity and the presence of additional certifications. **Superb Plastics Pvt. Ltd.** ranked highest due to its strong quality certifications (including IATF 16949) and clear indication of high-volume capacity.

## Ranking Methodology
Suppliers were scored and ranked based on the following weighted criteria:
*   **Capability Fit (0.35):** Alignment with required process (Injection Molding) and material (ABS), plus relevant complementary services.
*   **Certifications (0.20):** Presence of ISO 9001 and other relevant quality/environmental certifications.
*   **Capacity (0.20):** Ability to meet or exceed the minimum monthly capacity of 50,000 units, with preference for explicit statements.
*   **Lead Time (0.15):** (Currently unknown for all suppliers, contributing to gaps).
*   **Evidence Quality (0.10):** Number and quality of web sources supporting the claims.
