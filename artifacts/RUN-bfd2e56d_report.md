# Supplier Comparison Report

This report evaluates and ranks potential injection molding suppliers in India based on the specified requirements for ABS material, ISO 9001 certification, and a minimum monthly capacity of 50,000 units.

## Requirements Summary:
*   **Process:** Injection Molding
*   **Material:** ABS
*   **Location Preference:** India
*   **Certifications:** ISO 9001
*   **Monthly Capacity Min:** 50,000 units

## Ranking Methodology:
Suppliers are scored and ranked based on a weighted assessment across key criteria:
*   **Capability Fit (35%):** Alignment with core process (Injection Molding), material handling (ABS), and breadth of additional relevant services (e.g., tooling, design, assembly).
*   **Certifications (20%):** Presence of ISO 9001 and additional relevant certifications (e.g., IATF 16949, ISO 14001).
*   **Capacity (20%):** Ability to meet or exceed the minimum monthly capacity, with higher scores for more explicit and substantial capacity indicators.
*   **Lead Time (15%):** Not provided by any supplier in the dataset, resulting in a neutral score for all (0 points). This category did not differentiate suppliers in this evaluation.
*   **Evidence Quality (10%):** Number and quality of supporting web evidence.
